# FietsNav — promo reel & share kit

`reel.html` is a self-contained, vertical **9:16** animated promo (~27s, auto-plays + loops).
Open it, **screen-record** one full loop, and you have a clip for Instagram Reels/Stories or
WhatsApp status. The on-screen buttons fade out ~3s in, so they never land in the recording.

## How to record a clean video

### Fastest — on your Mac (recommended)
1. Open `promo/reel.html` in Chrome/Safari (double-click it, or drag into a browser tab).
2. Press **⇧⌘5** → choose **Record Selected Portion** → drag a box tight around the green
   9:16 reel → **Record**.
3. Let it play one full loop (~27s) → click ⏹ in the menu bar to stop.
4. The `.mov` lands on your Desktop. Drag it into Instagram/WhatsApp (they’ll crop to 9:16).

### On your phone
1. Get the file on the phone — AirDrop `reel.html` to your iPhone (opens in Safari), or
   deploy it and open `fietsnav.netlify.app/promo/reel.html`.
2. It auto-plays. Wait ~3s for the buttons to fade.
3. **iPhone:** swipe down from the top-right → tap ⏺ **Screen Recording**
   (add it via Settings → Control Center if missing).
   **Android:** swipe down twice → **Screen record** → Start.
4. Record one loop (~27s), then stop. The clip saves to Photos/Gallery.

> Tip for IG: add a trending audio track in the Instagram editor after uploading — the reel is
> intentionally silent so any music fits.
